About the Club

Initialized in 2013, Illinois Tech Esports is an established, non-profit collegiate esports organization that has quickly grown to be seen as a regional leader recognized for its community focus and high-quality events.

Our mission is to create a space for everyone to connect through casual and competitive gaming - we strive to create experiences dedicated to the player and aim to provide value for both gamers and our sponsors.

Events

Every semester, our club hosts a variety of events, such as:

  • LANs

  • Watch Parties

  • Tournaments

  • Socials
